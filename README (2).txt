This project analyzes Walmart retail sales data to uncover insights on customer behavior, sales trends, and branch performance using Python and visualization tools.

---

##  Project Overview

This dataset contains transactional sales data from three Walmart branches across different cities.  
The goal of this analysis is to extract meaningful business insights through data wrangling, EDA, and visualizations.

---

##  Objectives

- Analyze sales performance across branches
- Understand customer purchasing patterns
- Identify top-performing product lines
- Compare revenue by month, city, and customer type
- Analyze VAT (Tax) and rating trends
- Build visual summaries (bar charts, pie charts, heatmaps)

---

##  Tools & Libraries

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

##  Key Business Insights

- The **highest revenue** comes from specific months & branches
- **Members** or **Normal customers** differ in spending patterns
- **Product line performance** helps identify strong & weak segments
- **VAT contribution** varies by product type & city
- **Sales peak** at certain times of day & week
- Customer **ratings are higher** at specific times
